Energy Frame

Energy Frame is a dynamic and growing energy company whose business plan includes strategies to participate in several sectors of the natural resources industry including Oil and gas exploration, production, and development of new technologies for the enhancement of oil and gas production.

Experienced and Dedicated

We are attracting and retaining experienced and reliable technical professionals through performance-based incentives.​

  • The company offers expert knowledge and technology to oil and gas companies from the exploration to development phases in geology and geophysics, petroleum engineering, petroleum economics and environmental studies.
  • We provide reliable technical evaluations of oil and gas exploration and production business opportunities, and consultancy in oil developement including renewable energy.

Our Goals

Energy Frame wishes to specialize in the consulting of integrated renewable energy systems. Our goal is to provide environmental services. As such, we have teamed with some of the leading experts to provide total solutions to meet our clients' needs.

We believe green energy and the preservation of the environment is the key to solving the challenges created by the world economic crisis, green house gas emissions, and our dependence on fossil fuels.

Our Speciality

We specialize in projects for customers that use client data. Our experienced professionals have a deep understanding of key issues faced by oil and gas companies and future alternative energy, including asset portfolio optimizations, evolving E&P supply chains.

  • Energy Frame can produce, process, and distribute natural resources while providing geological studies and services to the oil & gas industry in response to the Energy market.
